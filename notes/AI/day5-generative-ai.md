# Day 5 - Generative AI Fundamentals

## What is Generative AI?

Generative AI is a branch of Artificial Intelligence that creates new content from patterns learned in data.

Unlike traditional AI, which mainly predicts or classifies, Generative AI can generate:

- Text
- Images
- Code
- Audio
- Videos

---

## Examples

- Microsoft Copilot
- ChatGPT
- GitHub Copilot
- DALL·E

---

## Real-world Applications

- Customer support
- Software development
- Education
- Healthcare
- Marketing
- Research

---

## Benefits

- Improves productivity
- Automates repetitive tasks
- Assists creativity
- Accelerates learning
